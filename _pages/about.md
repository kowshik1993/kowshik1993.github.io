<div style="text-align: justify;">
I am a Master of Science student in Cybersecurity at the University of Nebraska at Omaha (UNO), combining graduate research with more than eight years of professional experience in IT infrastructure, network security, compliance auditing, and enterprise systems management.
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
My research at UNO’s ASTRA Lab focuses on UAV-based acoustic sensing, particularly drone ego-noise suppression, deep-learning-based acoustic event detection, and microphone-array direction-of-arrival (DoA) estimation. This research has potential applications in public-safety monitoring, emergency response, infrastructure inspection, and other cyber-physical systems. My research was supported by a Graduate Research and Creative Activity (GRACA) Award from the University of Nebraska at Omaha. The funded project explores machine-learning-enabled noise suppression and robust acoustic-source tracking under severe drone ego-noise.
</div>
<div style="text-align: justify;">
I am also working on multichannel audio watermarking project. We dveloped a machine learning model that will encode watermark orthogonally in multichannel audio and will be able to protect the copywrite intigrity of the audio recording.
</div>
