Engineered an embedded leak-detection system using sensors and microcontrollers, with automated valve control and alerting to prevent common water loss. Developed as a sponsored project through Texas Instruments and Visvesvaraya Technological University (December 2015).

**Tools used:** Arduino IDE, Android Studio, Solenoid Valve, GSM 900, Ultrasonic Sensors

**How it works:** Ultrasonic sensors detect abnormal water flow or leaks, triggering a solenoid valve to automatically shut off the water supply. A GSM 900 module sends real-time alerts, and an Android app (built with Android Studio) allows for remote monitoring and control of the locking system.
