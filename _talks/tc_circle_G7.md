TSTECH Co., Ltd. is a global company across 14 countries. Every year there will be TC Circle competition where all branch of TSTECH will show their significant improvement in their location. There will be 3 round of competition one held in the branch, one in regional head office and final is in Japan headquater. I served as Team Leader for Team G7, representing TSECH Bangladesh Ltd. to the finalist round at the TC Circle World Convention 2024 held in Tokyo, Japan. We showed our improvement which have very significant profit for the company.
