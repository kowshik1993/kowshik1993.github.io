TSTECH Co., Ltd. is a global company across 14 countries. Every year there will be TC Circle competition where all branch of TSTECH will show their significant improvement in their location. There will be 3 round of competition one held in the branch, one in regional head office and final is in Japan headquater. I served as Team Leader for Team USA Trump, representing TSECH Bangladesh Ltd. to the asian head office round at the TC Asia Pacific Convention 2019 held in Bangkok, Thailand.
