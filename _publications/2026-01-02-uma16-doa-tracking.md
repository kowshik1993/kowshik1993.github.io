This project benchmarks direction-of-arrival (DoA) estimation using a UMA-16 microphone array in low-noise settings, evaluating accuracy and stability of source localization across a range of angles and distances. The results establish a baseline for source-tracking performance before introducing the added complexity of drone ego-noise.
