This project integrates the noise-reduction model with UMA-16 direction-of-arrival tracking, testing the combined system's reliability on a hovering drone. Controlled playback experiments — including simulated gunshot audio — evaluate how well the system localizes critical public-safety sounds despite severe drone ego-noise, directly supporting the goals of the GRACA-funded research.
